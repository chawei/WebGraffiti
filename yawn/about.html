About
How would an interface with its own consciousness, senses, and gestures reverse the passive role of user in Interaction Design?
"The Search Operators" is the sloppy twin of Google search. It opens up a narrative space inhabited by three sloppy Google operators - Jarek, Remo, and the yawning Surina - which allows people to speculate upon an emotional reality within technology.

This project is a mash-up of Html5 canvas, processing.js, jQuery, Google Search API and humanistic expressions: a radical response to patterns of interface design which predicts human behavior. By yuin chien and chawei hsu.

More projects: Anti-Like Magnet & Pixel Monster.
